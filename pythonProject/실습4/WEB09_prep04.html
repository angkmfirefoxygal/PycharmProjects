Italian Pasta Dish

This dish is a classic Italian recipe.

The pasta is cooked al dente and then mixed with a delicious tomato sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Pasta
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Basil
  • Red Pepper Flakes

This pasta dish is perfect for those who love spicy food.

To make this dish, cook the pasta according to the package instructions. In a separate pan, saute garlic and onions in olive oil until soft. Add diced tomatoes and red pepper flakes, and let the sauce simmer for 10-15 minutes. Toss the cooked pasta with the sauce, top with fresh basil and parmesan cheese, and serve hot.

Last but not least, don't forget to garnish with some parmesan cheese!
